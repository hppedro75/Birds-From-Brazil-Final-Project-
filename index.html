The Amazing Birds of Brazil

Brazil has many beautiful and diverse bird species that
range in terms of size, color and type. The many types of
locations these birds come from make them really different.

Here is a list of some interesting birds from Brazil:

  1. Blue Macaw

    These are some of the rarest and most beautiful birds of them all

  2. Black Anu

    These are beautiful strong beaked birds

  3. White Anu

    These birds have many names around Brazil, like "cat's soul" for an example.

  4. Jacu or "Penelope"

    These big birds can reach up to 85cm in length. They popularly are known as 'Jacus' in Brazil, though their scientic gender is "Penelope"

  5. Curiaca-de-Colar

    These tiny birds are also known as 'Alarm Clocks' in Pantanal - Brazil
